# Switch Environments

Attune automatically detects environments in your workspace:

- **Node.js**: `package.json`, `.nvmrc`
- **Python**: `venv`, `.venv`, `env`

## How to Switch

1. Click the **Attune** status bar item (bottom left).
2. Or run **Attune: Switch Environment** from the Command Palette.
3. Select your desired environment.

Attune will create a new terminal and activate the environment for you!
